About DNZ Resources

Trading Surplus.
Creating Value.

Since 1960, DNZ Resources has specialised in the global trade of second-choice and waste paper, board, pulp and flexible packaging materials. Operating from our main base in Germany and our office in Sharjah, UAE, we bridge the gap between manufacturers with surplus and end users who can put materials back to work.

Our edge is 60 years of accumulated knowledge — understanding grades, specifications, and market dynamics that most traders simply don't have.

Our Purpose

Every Sheet Deserves
a Second Chance

Every year, millions of tonnes of perfectly usable paper and packaging material are written off — not because they're bad, but because an order was cancelled, a specification changed, or production ran over. That material has a life left in it.

This is where DNZ Resources steps in. We connect manufacturers who have surplus with buyers who can use it — giving materials a second life, reducing waste, and keeping tonnes of product out of landfill every single year.

It's not just good business. It's the right thing to do for our planet — and it has been our purpose since 1960.
